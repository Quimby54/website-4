About Cute Puppies

About

I love my dog, and even though she's 2 years old she still behaves and looks like a puppy

Because of her I wanted to base this project on puppies.
Their loyalty, playfulness and little quirks can make a huge difference in someones day, and does so for mine without fail.
Each dog that I've had the past 27 years has had a huge impact on my life, and has made me a person that deeply loves their pet.
